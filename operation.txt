Operation
1. Open the program by switching on the power source.
2. The device will set all sensors while the system is initializing.
3. The device will send a message containing “System Ready” to the owner.
4. The device will display real time readings of temperature and gas level.
5. The owner can send SMS to the device to get system status.
6. When the temperature reaches 70⁰C and the gas sensor rating reaches 800 out of 1023 the alarm will activate.
7. The system will send SMS to the owner containing “The house in on fire” together with the status.
8. Press the red button to stop the alarm.
9. You can also terminate the alarm by sending SMS to the system.
